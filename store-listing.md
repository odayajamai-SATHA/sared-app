## Description (English)

Sared is the fastest way to get a flatbed tow truck or roadside assistance in Saudi Arabia.

🚗 SERVICES
• Flatbed tow truck (standard, hydraulic, enclosed)
• Flat tire change
• Battery jump start
• Fuel delivery
• Car lockout
• Winch & vehicle recovery
• Inter-city vehicle transport

⚡ WHY SARED?
• Request in seconds — no complex registration
• Guaranteed transparent pricing — see the maximum price before you confirm
• Real-time driver tracking on the map
• Pay your way — Cash, STC Pay, or Mada
• 24/7 availability
• Arabic & English support

🛡️ SAFETY FIRST
• All drivers carry liability insurance
• Emergency SOS button with live location sharing
• Rate your driver after every service

📍 AVAILABLE IN
Eastern Province (Dammam, Khobar, Dhahran) — expanding soon

Proudly made in Saudi Arabia 🇸🇦
